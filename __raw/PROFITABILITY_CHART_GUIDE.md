## **Data Interpretation Guide**

### **What Each Metric Shows:**

**Return on Equity (ROE) - Primary Axis:**
- **WestJet: 16.4%** â† WINNER
- Measures: How efficiently company uses shareholder equity
- Interpretation: WestJet generates 16.4 cents profit per dollar of equity
- **DOUBLE Southwest (3.6%), nearly DOUBLE Lufthansa (8.7%)**

**Return on Assets (ROA) - Primary Axis:**
- **WestJet: 5.4%** â† WINNER
- Measures: How efficiently company uses all assets
- Interpretation: WestJet generates 5.4 cents profit per dollar of assets
- **4.5x better than Southwest (1.2%)**

**Operating Margin - Secondary Axis:**
- **WestJet: 11.5%** â† WINNER
- Measures: Operational efficiency before interest/taxes
- Interpretation: For every revenue dollar, 11.5 cents is operating profit
- **2.8x better than Southwest (4.1%), 6.8x better than Lufthansa (1.7%)**

**Net Profit Margin - Secondary Axis:**
- **WestJet: 7.0%** â† TIED FOR WINNER (with Singapore 7.2%)
- Measures: Bottom-line profitability
- Interpretation: For every revenue dollar, 7 cents reaches shareholders
- **4.4x better than Southwest (1.6%), 2.9x better than Lufthansa (2.4%)**
